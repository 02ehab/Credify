Problem

Credit in emerging markets is opaque, fragmented, and reactive. Consumers lack visibility, lenders lack signals.

Low Credit Literacy

Most consumers don’t know what drives scores or how to improve them. Small mistakes have outsized consequences.

Reactive Disputes

Errors and fraud are discovered late. Resolution is manual, slow, and expensive for both users and bureaus.

Thin Files

Limited data means worthy borrowers are mispriced or rejected. Lenders struggle to acquire and retain safely.

Solution

A consumer-first Credit OS that monitors, explains, and improves credit — while generating verified, real-time signals for partners.
